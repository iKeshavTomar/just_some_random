Dear Sakshi,


I'm not great at saying the big things out loud. You know that.

I'm the guy who deflects with a joke when things get too real.

But here's the thing — you make me want to be better at this.


You make the ordinary feel special. You make me want to pay

attention. To remember the small things — like the way you look

when you're trying not to laugh at my terrible jokes, or the way

you roll your eyes but still hold my hand.


I don't know when exactly this started. There was no lightning

bolt moment. It was more like... waking up one day and realizing

I'd been walking toward you for a while, and I just didn't want

to stop.


So yeah. This is me, saying it. In a website. With rose petals.

Very cool, very normal behavior.


Don't get a big ego about this. It took like five minutes.
